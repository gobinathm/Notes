# Privacy Policy

*Last Updated: January 14, 2026*

## Our Commitment to Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. This site is designed with privacy as a priority.

## What We Track

We use **Umami Analytics** (hosted in the EU) to understand how visitors use our study notes. We collect:

- **Page views** - Which study materials are most helpful
- **Referrer sources** - How you found our site
- **Country** - General geographic location (not city-level)
- **Device type** - Desktop, mobile, or tablet

## What We DON'T Track

- ❌ **No personal information** - We don't know who you are
- ❌ **No cookies** - We don't store anything on your device
- ❌ **No IP addresses** - We don't store identifying information
- ❌ **No cross-site tracking** - We don't follow you around the internet
- ❌ **No selling data** - We never share or sell any data

## Why We Track

We track basic usage statistics to:
- Understand which certification materials are most helpful
- Improve study content based on what's popular
- Know where to focus our efforts

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to delete or access. You're completely anonymous when visiting this site.

## Analytics Provider

We use [Umami Analytics](https://umami.is/), an open-source, privacy-focused analytics tool:
- GDPR compliant
- No cookies
- Data stored in EU
- Anonymous by design

## External Links

This site links to external resources (AWS, GitHub, etc.). Those sites have their own privacy policies.

open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/gobinathm/Notes/issues).
