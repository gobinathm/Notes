# About These Notes

These are my personal study notes as I prepare for various technology certifications. I'm documenting what I learn and sharing it publicly in case it helps others on their certification journey.

## What You'll Find

**Structured Content:**
- Notes organized by official exam objectives
- Code examples and commands
- Links to official resources
- Study tips and strategies

**Exam Preparation:**
- Exam traps and common mistakes
- Decision matrices for "which option is best" questions
- Last-minute review cheat sheets
- Performance and security best practices

**Progress Tracking:**
- Personal progress tracker (stored locally in your browser)
- Your progress is private and never leaves your device

2. **Study the Notes** - Deep dive into detailed notes with examples
3. **Review Exam Tips** - Learn common traps and exam strategies
4. **Track Progress** - Use the progress tracker to monitor your learning
5. **Practice** - Hands-on practice is crucial for most technical certifications
