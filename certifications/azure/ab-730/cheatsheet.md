## Responsible AI (R.A.P)

- **R**eview — Always verify AI output
- **A**ccuracy — Check for fabrications
- **P**rivacy — No training on business data

---

## Quick Decision Rules

**AI seems wrong?**
→ Hallucination. Check citations.

**Need specific company data?**
→ Grounding (Graph). Use `/` to link files.

**Need to collaborate on AI output?**
→ Copilot Pages (Canvas).

**Need to reuse high-quality prompts?**
→ Copilot Lab (Gallery).

**Need special business logic?**
→ Copilot Agents.

---

## Terminology Check

- **Microsoft Graph**: The "brain" connecting your files/emails to AI.
- **Grounding**: Providing context to keep the AI accurate.
- **Fabrication**: Confidence in a lie (Hallucination).
- **Tenant**: Your private organization boundary in Azure/M365.
