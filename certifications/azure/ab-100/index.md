# AB-100: Microsoft Agentic AI Business Solutions Architect

An advanced Microsoft certification for solution architects who design, deploy, govern, and optimize AI-powered business solutions using Microsoft Copilot, Copilot Studio, Microsoft Foundry, Dynamics 365, and Power Platform.

<ExamMeta duration="100 min" questions="TBD" passing="700/1000" cost="Varies by region" level="Advanced" provider="Pearson VUE" validity="12 months" />

::: warning Currently Studying
**Target Date**: TBD

**Notes Prepared**: May 2026

**Last Updated**: 2026-05-13

**Notes valid as of**: May 13, 2026
:::

::: info Architect mindset
AB-100 is scenario-heavy. Think like an AI-first solution architect: align to business outcomes, design secure and governable agents, select the right Microsoft platform, and plan for ALM, telemetry, testing, ROI, and responsible AI.
:::
