## Microsoft AI Solutions

| Need | Solution |
|------|----------|
| Knowledge workers | **M365 Copilot** |
| Custom AI apps | **Azure AI Foundry** |
| CRM/ERP | Dynamics 365 Copilot |
| Low-code | Power Platform AI |

## M365 Copilot Apps

| App | Does |
|-----|------|
| Word | Draft, summarize, rewrite |
| Excel | Analyze, formulas, charts |
| PowerPoint | Create, design |
| Outlook | Draft, summarize, prioritize |
| Teams | Meeting notes, actions |

---

## Copilot Security (TRP)

- **T**enant isolation — data stays yours
- **R**espects permissions — sees what you can see
- **P**rivate — not used for training

---

## 6 Responsible AI Principles

**FRPITA**

| | |
|-|-|
| **F**airness | Treat all equitably |
| **R**eliability | Perform consistently |
| **P**rivacy | Protect data |
| **I**nclusiveness | Work for everyone |
| **T**ransparency | Be understandable |
| **A**ccountability | People own decisions |

---

## Adoption Phases

1. **Pilot** (4-8 wks) — 50-100 champions
2. **Expand** (8-12 wks) — more departments
3. **Deploy** (ongoing) — organization-wide

---

## What Comes First?

- ✅ Executive sponsorship
- ✅ Governance & policies
- ✅ Readiness assessment
- ✅ Pilot

- ❌ NOT technical deployment
- ❌ NOT broad rollout

---

## AI Council = ELBIT

- **E**xecutive sponsor
- **L**egal/compliance
- **B**usiness units
- **I**T leadership
- **T**raining/HR

---

## Success Metrics

- Adoption (% active users)
- Productivity (time saved)
- Quality (errors reduced)
- Satisfaction (user score)
- Business (ROI)

---

## Quick Decision Rules

**"Which solution?"**
→ Knowledge worker = M365 Copilot
→ Custom AI = Azure AI Foundry

**"What first?"**
→ Governance, not technical setup

**"Security concerns?"**
→ Data private, permissions respected

**"Measure success?"**
→ All 5 categories, not just adoption
